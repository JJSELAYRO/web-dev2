Our Founder

Jazel Jade H. Selayro

Visionary Educator and Entrepreneur

Jazel Jade H. Selayro founded Tamad Kaba University in 2010 with a vision to revolutionize higher education in the Philippines. His innovative approach to learning combines academic with practical skills development, preparing students for the challenges of the 21st century.

"Tamad Kaba Ayaw Ko Sa Tamad !!"

Under his leadership, TKU has grown from a small institution to one of the most respected universities in the country, recognized for its academic excellence and commitment to student success.
